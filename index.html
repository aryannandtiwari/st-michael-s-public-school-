About Us

St. Michael's Public School is a premier English Medium Co-Educational Institution located in Thakurgaon on School Road, Ranchi. Since 2014, under the management of Bright Future Educational Trust (Regd.), the school has provided quality education while nurturing discipline, character and academic excellence.
