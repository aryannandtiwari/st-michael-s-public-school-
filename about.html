About Us

St. Michael's Public School is a premier English Medium Co-Educational Institution located in Thakurgaon, on School Road. Since its establishment in 2014, it has been functioning under the esteemed management of the Bright Future Educational Trust (Regd.) with a vision to provide excellent education to the students of the region.

Over the years, the school has earned a reputation for nurturing young minds with care, dedication, and a commitment to quality.

The school boasts a large strength of students and offers a rich curriculum beginning from Nursery, K.G., Prep (Play Group), and continues through Standard I to X, following the guidelines of the Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E.).

We emphasize academic excellence combined with the development of strong moral values.

Our motto is to build two fundamental pillars in every student's life: Discipline and Character which are essential for future success.

We also believe in providing quality education at affordable cost so that every child gets an opportunity to learn and grow.

Our Vision

At St. Michael's Public School, our vision is to empower students to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape by fostering creativity, resilience, and a strong sense of responsibility.

We aim to instill critical thinking skills, cultural awareness, and a deep respect for values such as integrity, empathy, perseverance, and respect for others.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a nurturing and dynamic learning environment where every student is encouraged to reach their full potential.

We are committed to delivering a well-rounded education that combines academic excellence with personal growth, ensuring our students are prepared to meet future challenges with confidence and compassion.
